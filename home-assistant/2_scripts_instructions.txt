================================================================================
           INSTRUCTION 2: CREATE THE SCRIPTS IN HOME ASSISTANT
================================================================================

You need to create **two scripts**:

1. enroll_new_fingerprint
2. delete_fingerprint

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SCRIPT 1: enroll_new_fingerprint
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. Go to **Settings → Automations & Scenes → Scripts**
2. Click **+ Create Script**
3. Click the three dots (⋮) in the top right → **Edit in YAML**
4. Delete everything and paste the contents of enroll-script.yaml
5. Click **Save Script**

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SCRIPT 2: delete_fingerprint
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. Click **+ Create Script** again
2. Click the three dots (⋮) → **Edit in YAML**
4. Delete everything and paste the contents of delete-script.yaml
5. Click **Save Script**
You now have two scripts ready to use from the dashboard.
