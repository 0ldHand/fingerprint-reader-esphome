================================================================================
           BATTERY-POWERED FINGERPRINT READER - WIRING INSTRUCTIONS
                    Seeed Xiao ESP32-C6 + Grow R503 Sensor
================================================================================

This guide covers the complete wiring for the ESPHome-based fingerprint reader
with power-saving mode, external antenna, and long battery life.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                           1. PINOUT SUMMARY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

R503 Fingerprint Sensor (6 wires):
  Red                = VCC (Main Power)     → Through PNP Transistor Collector
  Black              = GND                  → Common Ground
  Yellow             = TX (Data Out)        → GPIO17 (D7 on front)
  Green or Brown     = RX (Data In)         → GPIO16 (D6 on front)
  Blue               = Sensing (Touch)      → GPIO2  (D2 on front)
  White              = 3.3VT (Touch Power)  → Direct to 3V3 (bypass transistor)

PNP Transistor (BC327):
  Emitter   → 3V3 (Xiao)
  Collector → Red wire (R503 VCC)
  Base      → 10kΩ Resistor → GPIO19 (D8 on front)

Power:
  2× 18650 cells + BMS → Xiao battery pads (+ and - on back)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                        2. DETAILED WIRING TABLE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

| Component          | Wire / Pin          | Connect To                  | Notes |
|--------------------|---------------------|-----------------------------|-------|
| R503 Sensor        | Red (VCC)           | Transistor Collector        | Main power (switched) |
| R503 Sensor        | Black (GND)         | Common GND                  | - |
| R503 Sensor        | Yellow (TX)         | GPIO17 (D7)                 | UART RX on ESP32 |
| R503 Sensor        | Green/Brown (RX)    | GPIO16 (D6)                 | UART TX on ESP32 |
| R503 Sensor        | Blue (Sensing)      | GPIO2 (D2)                  | Touch detection |
| R503 Sensor        | White (3.3VT)       | 3V3 (Xiao)                  | Must stay powered |
| PNP Transistor     | Emitter             | 3V3 (Xiao)                  | Power source |
| PNP Transistor     | Collector           | Red wire (R503)             | Switched power |
| PNP Transistor     | Base                | 10kΩ Resistor → GPIO19 (D8) | Control pin |
| Battery Pack       | +                   | Xiao BAT+ (back)            | From BMS |
| Battery Pack       | -                   | Xiao GND (back)             | From BMS |

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                    3. STEP-BY-STEP WIRING INSTRUCTIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

STEP 1: Prepare the Transistor Circuit (Power Control)
----------------------------------------------------
1. Take the BC327 PNP transistor.
2. Solder a 10kΩ resistor to the Base leg.
3. Connect the other end of the 10k resistor to GPIO19 (D8 on front).
4. Connect the Emitter leg to 3V3 on the Xiao.
5. Connect the Collector leg to the Red wire of the R503 sensor.

STEP 2: Connect the R503 Sensor
-------------------------------
1. Connect White wire (3.3VT) directly to 3V3 on the Xiao.
2. Connect Black wire (GND) to any GND pad on the Xiao.
3. Connect Yellow wire (TX) to GPIO17 (D7 on front).
4. Connect Green wire (RX) to GPIO16 (D6 on front).
5. Connect Blue wire (Sensing) to GPIO2 (D2 on front).
6. Connect Red wire (VCC) to the Transistor Collector (from Step 1).

STEP 3: Connect External Antenna
--------------------------------
1. Connect u.FL antenna to the Xiao (if not already done).

STEP 4: Connect Battery Pack
----------------------------
1. Connect the output of the 18650 BMS to the battery pads on the back of the Xiao:
   - BMS + → Xiao BAT+
   - BMS - → Xiao GND (any ground pad)
2. Make sure the BMS is properly connected to the two 18650 cells in parallel.

STEP 5: Double-Check All Connections
------------------------------------
- White wire → Direct 3V3 (never switched)
- Red wire → Only through transistor
- No wires connected to 5V
- All GNDs are common

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

□ Red wire connected only through transistor Collector
□ Yellow → GPIO17 (D7)
□ Green/Br → GPIO16 (D6)

□ Transistor Base → 10k resistor → GPIO19 (D8)

□ No wires on 5V pin
□ All grounds common

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                              NOTES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- The White wire (3.3VT) MUST stay powered at all times for touch detection.
- Only the Red wire (VCC) is switched by the transistor for power saving.
- After wiring, flash the ESPHome config and test the sensor LED turns on when finger is pressed.
